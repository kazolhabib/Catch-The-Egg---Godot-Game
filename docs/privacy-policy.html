Catch The Eggs Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Catch The Eggs is a simple arcade game published by Mahade. This privacy policy explains how the game handles user information.

Information We Collect

Catch The Eggs does not collect, store, share, or sell personal information. The game does not require account sign-in and does not ask users to provide personal details.

Game Data

The game may store basic gameplay progress, such as high score information, locally on the user's device. This information stays on the device and is not sent to us or to third parties.

Ads and Third-Party Services

Catch The Eggs does not include advertising, analytics SDKs, in-app purchases, or third-party tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Catch The Eggs is not directed specifically to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If the game changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated before those changes are published.
